Max Temperature


The latitude is a geographic coordinate that indicates the North-South position of a particular point on the surface of the earth. All latitude lines are parallel, and they run from East to West as circles, which are parallel to the equator. Temperature is inversely related to latitude. As latitude increases, the temperature falls, and vice versa. As shown in above graph, There is clear realation between temperature and latitude. The weather becomes warmer as you approaches to the equator.
