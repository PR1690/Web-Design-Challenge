Cloudiness


This plot doesn't show any correlation. Scientifically, there is no evidence regarding latitude have a direct effect on cloudiness. Continents tend to be less cloudy than adjacent oceans (at the same latitude), except in the equatorial belt. On average, cloudiness is highest around 60deg Latitude (about 80% of the time) but there is only 40% cloud cover at the South Pole.
